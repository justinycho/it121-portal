About Me

Hi! My name is Justin Cho, I'm currently a student at Year Up who is eager to build professional connections with those in the tech industry.

In the Year Up program, I've been learning skills about SD&T, specifically QA. These skills include HTML/CSS, JavaScript, Microsoft Office 365, and various version control systems such as GitHub.

Prior to Year Up, I was working in the food service industry and at car dealerships. Through these experiences, I was able to develop soft skills such as time management, critical thinking, and team proficiency.

I'd like to place myself among experienced software engineers so I can gain experience and sharpen my skills. I'm very interested in developing, testing, and debugging software, specifically computer programs, applications, and games that will be adopted by general consumers.

I believe that inefficient and faulty software is the main conflict people face on day-to-day basis in our digitized world and I aim to confront this issue.
